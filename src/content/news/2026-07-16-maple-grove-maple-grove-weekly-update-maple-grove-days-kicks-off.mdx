This week Maple Grove celebrates its signature community festival while the city continues major investments in recreation infrastructure. Maple Grove Days runs through Sunday with events, concerts, and community traditions. Meanwhile, the city is moving forward with the second phase of its Community Center expansion project, and residents can look forward to new retail options at The Shoppes at Arbor Lakes in the coming months.

Maple Grove Days is underway, bringing five days of celebration to the community.
