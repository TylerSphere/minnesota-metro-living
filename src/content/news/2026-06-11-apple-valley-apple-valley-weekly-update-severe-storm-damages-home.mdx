This week in Apple Valley brought a mix of weather emergencies, milestone celebrations, and continued progress on development projects across the city. Severe storms swept through Dakota County early Wednesday morning, causing damage to at least one residence. Meanwhile, the community recently celebrated the Apple Valley High School Class of 2026 graduation, and several business and housing developments continue moving forward.

## Severe Storms Damage Apple Valley Home

A line of strong to severe thunderstorms moved through the Twin Cities metro area during the early morning hours of Wednesday, June 10, bringing powerful winds and hail that caused damage across portions of the region.
