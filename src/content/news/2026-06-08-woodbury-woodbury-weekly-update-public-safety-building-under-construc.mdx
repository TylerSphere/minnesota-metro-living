This week in Woodbury, residents are adjusting to temporary changes as major infrastructure projects advance across the city. The Public Safety building construction has prompted emergency services to relocate, while the city celebrates positive results from its biennial resident survey. Summer also brings the return of beloved community programs, including the East Metro Miracle League.
