This week brings positive fiscal news for Hopkins Public Schools and a busy stretch ahead for the community. The school district approved its 2027 budget projecting a near $2 million surplus following significant cost reductions. Meanwhile, residents are preparing for the 92nd annual Hopkins Raspberry Festival, which begins July 15.

## Hopkins Schools Projects $2 Million Budget Surplus for 2027

After a challenging fiscal year, Hopkins Public Schools is ending the 2026 school year on solid financial footing.
