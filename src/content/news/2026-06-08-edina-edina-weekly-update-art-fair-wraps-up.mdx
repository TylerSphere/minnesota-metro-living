This week brought a mix of community celebration and unexpected wildlife encounters to Edina. The 59th annual Edina Art Fair transformed the 50th and France district into a bustling arts destination, while city officials addressed questions about a bear spotted in the community. Meanwhile, the School Board is preparing to vote on a significant decision about the district's Spanish Dual Language Program.
