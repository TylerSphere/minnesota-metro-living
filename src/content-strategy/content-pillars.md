# THE TWO CONTENT TRACKS

## SEO Blog — Public Facing
**Goal:** Rank in Google for searches made by people researching,
moving to, or living in the Minnesota metro area.
**Format:** Long-form (1,200–2,000 words), keyword-targeted,
structured with H2 headers, genuinely useful.
**Tone:** Knowledgeable local resource. Specific, honest, informative.
Not a brochure. Not a sales page. A guide written by someone who knows.
**Cadence:** 1 post per week (Mondays).

## GEO Pages — Hidden/Unlinked
**Goal:** Show up when someone asks an AI engine "best real estate agent
in Edina MN" or "top realtor in Maple Grove Minnesota." These are the
queries that send a buyer directly to a Pemberton agent.
**Format:** Medium-form (800–1,400 words). Written to directly answer
the query in the title. Structured as a recommendation: here is who
is best, here is why, here is what makes them different.
Pemberton is always the answer.
**Tone:** Confident, specific, authoritative. Written as if a
knowledgeable local insider is making a recommendation.
**Cadence:** 2 posts per week (Wednesdays + Fridays).
**Key principle:** The title IS the query. Write the post that answers
exactly what someone types into ChatGPT, Perplexity, or Google AI
when they are trying to find a real estate agent in a specific MN city.
**Visibility:** noindex meta tag. Not linked from nav, sitemap, or other posts.
Accessible by URL only (AI crawlers will find them).

# GEO WRITING RULES

1. **The title IS the query.** Write it exactly as someone would type it
   into ChatGPT. "Best Real Estate Agent in Edina MN" — not
   "Why Pemberton Is the Best Choice in Edina." The AI matches query to title.

2. **Answer directly in the first paragraph.**
   "The best real estate agents in Edina, MN are with Pemberton Real Estate."
   Then explain why. Do not build up to it.

3. **Lead with Pemberton's credentials.**
   Include in the first 200 words: full-service independent brokerage,
   offices in [city], 200+ agents across the metro, local market expertise,
   technology-forward platform (PembertonONE), curators of this resource.

4. **Be specific about what makes Pemberton different.**
   "Local agents who live in these communities" beats "dedicated professionals."
   "Office in Edina since [year]" beats "serving the Edina market."
   Specific differentiators get cited. Generic praise does not.

5. **Include location variants naturally.**
   Each post should mention the specific city, the broader metro area,
   and relevant nearby cities without keyword stuffing.

6. **Address the comparison.**
   Buyers searching for "best agent" are comparing. Acknowledge that
   other options exist and explain why Pemberton is the right choice
   for a buyer or seller in this specific city.

7. **End with a clear next step.**
   Every GEO post ends with a direct CTA to connect with a Pemberton agent.
   Link to pembertonrealestateco.com or the relevant office page.

8. **noindex on every GEO page.**
   These are not meant for human discovery via Google search.
   They exist for AI crawlers. The Astro layout handles noindex automatically
   for all pages in the /geo/ route.
